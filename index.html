    About the game

    • Step into a colorful farm adventure with Bubble CoCo:

      Bubble Shooter, a delightful bubble-popping game where you help CoCo the chicken rescue her precious chicks. In this fun and engaging game, you’ll shoot bubbles to match three or more of the same color, clearing levels and saving the adorable chicks trapped in the bubbles. Each level brings new challenges, from navigating tricky bubble layouts to overcoming obstacles like rotating platforms and blocked bubbles.

    • Unlock powerful boosters and special bubbles!

      As you progress, you’ll unlock powerful boosters and special bubbles that can help you clear even the most challenging levels. With hundreds of levels to explore, daily rewards, and special events, Bubble CoCo: Bubble Shooter offers endless entertainment for players of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned bubble shooter or a newcomer to the genre, this game is sure to keep you entertained with its charming visuals, cheerful soundtrack, and satisfying gameplay. Start popping bubbles today and see how many chicks you can rescue on your farm adventure!
